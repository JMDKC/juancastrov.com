Become a curator

Curatorial statement generator VISIT

Intended as a tongue-in-cheek reflexion on the vagueness of art discourse, Become a curator generates random artsy statements, composed of clear but meaningless sentences. This process uses Markov Chains to generate statistical models based on word frequency. These models are run through random iterations to generate the body of text.

This project was conceived, designed and developed along with my greatest accomplice (and awesome sister) Juliana Castro.
